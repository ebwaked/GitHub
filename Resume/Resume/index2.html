Experience

The Beginning

I started college at UNC-Pembroke. I was a student athlete there for 2 years and I am very proud to have been apart of their innaugural football team. I decided to transfer to NC State where I pursued a BS in Biology with a concentration in Plant Biology. After NC State I became a financial Service Representative for MetLife Premier Client Group in Greensboro. I was a junior parntner in the agency but after spending 2 and a half years in the financial services industry I wanted to pursue what my real passion and hobby was; web developement.

Why now?

There are a few reasons why I left a potential career in financial services. I had a knack for understanding concepts and learning to implement good business habits into my practice, yet despite the designations and licences obtained during my time in the industry I never felt truly happy. While I enjoyed it at the time and have no regrets taking that path early on, I believe it allowed me the vision to pursue what I truly enjoy.

How I will Benefit YOU

My experience prior to joining Coder Foundry is limited to an HTML class and an Introduction to Java class taken at NC State in 2010 but during my time at Coder Foundry I can say I am one hundred percent sure I chose the right path. The fast past enviroment pushes me to the limit of what you can do and sets the bar high with the amount of information learned in a short period of time. I learned how to look at a problem, break it down into managable problems and implement those solutions with clean, clear and maintainable code. Over the course of this class we will learn a variety of different things which includes: MVC, AngularJS, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, JQuery, C#, SQL, Web API, MicroORM's, and InsightDB.
